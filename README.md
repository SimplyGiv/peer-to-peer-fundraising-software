# What is Peer-to-Peer Fundraising?

Peer-to-peer fundraising allows supporters to create their own fundraising pages and raise money on behalf of a nonprofit organization.

Instead of relying only on the nonprofit, individual supporters ask friends and family to donate.

Benefits include

- Increased donor reach
- Higher fundraising revenue
- More community engagement
- Better donor retention
- Increased brand awareness

---

# Features to Look For

A good peer-to-peer fundraising platform should include:

- Individual fundraising pages
- Team fundraising
- Online donation forms
- Mobile-friendly donation pages
- Social media sharing
- Event fundraising
- Recurring donations
- Fundraising thermometer
- Email receipts
- Reporting dashboard
